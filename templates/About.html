What Is BrainWaveGames?

BrainWave Games is a website targeted for children that develops their cognitive skills using specially developed video games on the website. The website will give you the ability to track your progress and to evaluate what type of skills might need further training and development.

The Goal

My website's goal is to develop young children's cognitive skills by playing specially developed video games on the website.

BrainWave Games brings education & video games into one simple place.
